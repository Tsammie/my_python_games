Dear Bears,

Thank you so much for letting me stay at your house I had a wonderful time and you were very kind to me. However, I am sorry that I ate Little Bear's porridge, broke his chair and fell asleep in his bed. I didn't mean to make him upset and cry. I hope he is feeling better about things now. I would love to come and stay with you again soon.
Best wishes,

Goldilocks
